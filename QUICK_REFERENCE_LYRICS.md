# 🎵 Lyrics Generator - Quick Reference Guide

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Open the app**: http://127.0.0.1:5001/lyrics-generator
2. **Fill in the form**:
   - Enter a theme (e.g., "Love", "Freedom", "Dreams")
   - Select your preferences
3. **Click "Generate Lyrics"**
4. **View, Copy, Download, or Share** your lyrics!

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

### 1. **Theme Selection**
✅ **Good themes:**
- Specific emotions: "Heartbreak", "Joy", "Freedom"
- Concepts: "Dreams", "Time", "Memories"
- Objects/People: "Ocean", "Mother", "City Lights"

❌ **Avoid:**
- Too generic: "Something", "Stuff"
- Too long: "The feeling I get when..."

### 2. **Genre + Mood Combinations**

**Best Matches:**
- Pop + Happy/Romantic
- Rock + Energetic/Angry
- R&B + Romantic/Calm
- Hip-Hop + Empowering/Energetic
- Blues + Sad/Melancholic
- EDM + Energetic/Happy
- Country + Nostalgic/Romantic
- Metal + Angry/Empowering

### 3. **Verse Count Guidelines**
- **2 Verses**: Radio-friendly, catchy, easy to remember
- **3 Verses**: Standard song length, tells a complete story
- **4 Verses**: Epic narratives, detailed storytelling

### 4. **Rhyme Scheme Selection**

**By Genre:**
- **Pop/Rock**: AABB or ABAB (classic, catchy)
- **Hip-Hop/Rap**: AABB or Free Verse
- **Country**: ABAB or ABCB (traditional)
- **Indie/Alternative**: Free Verse or ABCB
- **EDM**: AAAA (repetitive, hook-focused)

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### Issue: Lyrics don't generate
**Solutions:**
- Check theme is filled in
- Verify internet connection
- Refresh page and try again

### Issue: Similar lyrics each time
**Solution:**
- Lyrics use randomization - try clicking multiple times
- Change mood or genre for variety
- Use different themes

### Issue: Copy/Share not working
**Solutions:**
- Check browser permissions for clipboard
- Use download as alternative
- Try different browser
